# RamWeather: Hyper-Local Weather at Your Fingertips

**RamWeather** is a lightweight, intuitive weather application designed to give you the most accurate atmospheric data with zero friction. No clutter, no complicated maps—just the information you need to plan your day.

---

## 🔍 Core Feature: Instant City Search

The heart of RamWeather is its powerful search engine. Whether you're at home or planning a trip across the globe, getting your forecast is as simple as typing a name.

* **Global Coverage:** Access weather data for over 200,000 cities worldwide.
* **Predictive Input:** Start typing, and the app suggests cities instantly to save you keystrokes.
* **One-Tap Results:** Enter your city in the search bar and immediately view a comprehensive breakdown of current conditions.

---

## 🛠 Key Features

* **Real-Time Updates:** Get live data on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and UV index.
* **7-Day Forecast:** Look ahead with daily high/low predictions and expected precipitation.
* **Dynamic Backgrounds:** The app interface subtly shifts colors and themes to match the current weather state in your searched city.
* **Saved Locations:** Quickly toggle between your favorite cities without needing to search twice.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Launch** the RamWeather app.
2. **Locate** the search bar at the top of the interface.
3. **Enter your city** (e.g., "New York," "London," or "Tokyo").
4. **Press Enter** or select from the dropdown.
5. **View** your personalized weather dashboard instantly.
